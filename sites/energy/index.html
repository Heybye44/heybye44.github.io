Energy is measured (SI Unit) in joules and there are 2 main categories of energy. Click one to learn more.

  • Potential Energy
  • Kinetic Energy

Law of Conservation of Energy

Energy cannot be created nor destroyed. It can only be converted into another form. For example, a most light bulbs convert electricity into heat and light. This is called energy conversion.

Mechanical Energy

This is a type of energy, but it does not fall under one single category. Thus it has been placed here.
Mechanical energy is a combination of both kinetic and potential energy. It can be calculated as the sum of both types.
